📖 Overview
Agriculture remains a primary source of livelihood in many developing nations. Farmers often face challenges such as unpredictable weather conditions, pest infestations, crop diseases, soil degradation, and fluctuating market prices. These challenges can lead to poor decision-making, reduced crop yields, and financial losses.
The AI-Powered Farmer Advisory and Crop Price Forecasting System is an intelligent platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning, and Image Processing to provide comprehensive support for farmers. The system helps farmers make data-driven decisions through crop recommendations, weather forecasting, disease detection, and crop price prediction.

🎯 Problem Statement
Farmers often lack access to timely agricultural advisory services and advanced technological tools. This limitation affects crop selection, disease management, weather preparedness, and market planning.This project addresses these challenges by providing a unified AI-powered platform that delivers:
1.Smart crop recommendations
2.Weather forecasting and alerts
3.Rice leaf disease detection
4.Crop market price forecasting

✨ Features:

🌱 Crop Recommendation System
Recommends suitable crops based on weather conditions and historical agricultural data.Uses machine learning algorithms trained on climate and crop yield datasets.Helps improve productivity and resource utilization.

🌦️ Weather Forecast & Alert System
Predicts weather conditions and environmental risks.
Provides alerts for:
i.Rainfall variability
ii.Drought conditions
iii.Storms and adverse weather events
iv.Supports proactive farming decisions.

🍃 Rice Leaf Disease Detection
Uses deep learning and image processing techniques.
Detects and classifies rice leaf diseases including:
i.Brown Spot
ii.Leaf Smut
iii.Bacterial Leaf Blight
iv.Provides treatment and prevention recommendations.

📈 Crop Price Forecasting & Market Administration
Displays real-time crop market prices.Forecasts future crop prices using machine learning models.Helps farmers make profitable selling decisions.Improves market transparency.

📊 Modules Description

Module 1: Crop Recommendation Using Weather Data
This module analyzes environmental conditions and recommends crops that are best suited for the current climate and soil conditions.

Module 2: Weather Forecast and Alert System
Provides weather predictions and alerts to help farmers prepare for environmental challenges.

Module 3: Rice Leaf Disease Prediction
Processes leaf images using deep learning models to identify diseases and suggest remedies.

Module 4: Market Administration and Crop Price Forecasting
Offers real-time market information and predicts future crop prices for better financial planning.
